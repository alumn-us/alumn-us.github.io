Quick and Easy Setup

Alumn-Us is dedicated to saving you valuable time. Set up your alumni network in just a few hours with our straightforward three-step process:

Just upload your school logo, customize the theme colors, and input your school details. In less than 15 minutes, you'll have a fully branded alumni portal tailored to your institution.

With our user-friendly templates, you can quickly add events, fundraisers, news, and photos—each item only takes a few minutes to set up.

Easily import your alumni using our simple or advanced templates, based on the amount of data you wish to import. Alumni will receive an email invitation to join the network via Facebook, LinkedIn, Google, or by creating a custom profile with an email and password.

Watch Video

Events

Effortlessly set up events in minutes with our streamlined template. Alumni can register and make real-time credit card payments. Track attendance and send targeted emails to confirmed attendees.

Alumni database directory

Start creating a valuable alumni database. When alumni sign in via Facebook, LinkedIn, or Google, Alumnly automatically syncs their available information. They’re then prompted to complete a detailed profile covering their contact details, education, career history, achievements, and—most importantly—how they can contribute to the network. This may include offering mentorship, career guidance, sharing work experience, and more.

Fundraising

Easily set up fundraising events and items with real-time payment processing through Stripe. Set your fundraising goal and keep the community engaged with live progress updates as you move toward your target.

Email Newsletters

Take advantage of powerful built-in email marketing tools. Easily send messages to specific groups by graduation year, level of alumni engagement, custom lists, or your entire database. Say goodbye to third-party services—email campaigns are now simpler than ever!

News & Photo Albums

Effortlessly share news and photo albums with our easy-to-use templates. Alumni can also upload photos, leave comments, and like posts. Choose between manual or automatic moderation to maintain control over content.

Social / Directory

Alumnly functions as a genuine social network for alumni. Members can search the database by graduation year, profession, name, or by the ways others are open to offering support. They can also engage by posting updates, commenting, and liking content.

School Dashboard

Get a quick overview of your network’s performance—see total alumni registrations, active members, funds raised through events and fundraising, new connections formed, and much more.

Business Directory

Give alumni and community members a platform to promote their businesses. Set custom pricing for standard and premium listings, with the option for recurring payments. It’s a powerful new way to boost your fundraising efforts!

Jobs

Showcase job openings at your institution and empower alumni to share opportunities from their own workplaces. There’s no better place to find top talent than within your own trusted network!

Shop

Easily sell products to alumni with a built-in shopping cart and real-time payment gateway. Set straightforward shipping rules and manage order fulfillment directly from the CMS to keep everything on track.
